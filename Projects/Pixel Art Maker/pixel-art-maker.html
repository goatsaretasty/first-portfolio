Welcome to Pixel Art Maker (PAM for short)!

The Paint 2D option allows you to specify the height and width of a grid, and then draw on it by picking from the provided palette. You can click and drag to color cells, and erase using your mouse's right-click functionality.

To clear a grid but keep its width and height, click the Clear Grid button.

To draw a new grid, simly re-enter the columns and rows, and then click Draw It! again.

Pixy Paint is a simple paint canvas where you can pick a color and brush size to create your next masterpiece.
